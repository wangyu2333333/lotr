These are evil days for Gondor. Again and again I hear rumours of faithless traitors who have defected to the cities of the South, doubtless under some false promise of wealth or glory from the Enemy. And these renegades, I am told, gather chiefly in the old city of Umbar. If you should hunt down $ and execute them, the reward shall be great.
Have you heard of the renegades gathering in Umbar, #? It pains me to take up arms against those who were once my fellow countrymen, but we cannot have those traitors passing secrets to the Southrons! My captains have offered great reward to anyone who would pursue and put to death $.
